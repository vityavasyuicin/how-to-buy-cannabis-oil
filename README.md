## Understanding Cannabis Oil
Cannabis oil, derived from the Cannabis sativa or indica plant, contains cannabinoids like CBD and THC. It's popular for potential wellness benefits, such as pain relief, anxiety reduction, and skincare. In 2026, with evolving global regulations, buying cannabis oil legally has become more accessible in many places. However, sourcing it safely requires caution to avoid counterfeit or contaminated products.

### Types of Cannabis Oil
- **CBD Oil**: Non-psychoactive, often used for everyday wellness. Legal in most countries with hemp-derived versions.
- **THC Oil**: Psychoactive; availability depends on medical or recreational legalization.
- **Full-Spectrum vs. Isolate**: Full-spectrum includes multiple cannabinoids for the \entourage effect,\ while isolates are pure CBD.

For non-THC options, buy from health stores or online from hemp farms. The 2018 US Farm Bill (and similar global rules) allows hemp CBD with <0.3% THC.
